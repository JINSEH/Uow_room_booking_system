About Us

Overview

The Tay Eng Soon Library at SIM Campus supports the professional development and lifelong learning of our community through both physical and electronic resources. The library has been made shelf-less to provide an easier way to borrow books and make way for fresh new spaces such as a café-style seating area, privacy pods, group discussion rooms, Chill Corner, and Silent Zone.

Vision

A library that brings people together by delivering an inspiring library experience through smarter use of technology and a comprehensive resource pool to support learning needs.
